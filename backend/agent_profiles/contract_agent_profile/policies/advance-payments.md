# Advance Payment Review Guidance

## Rule

Payment should be tied to verifiable delivery, installation, acceptance, and performance milestones. High advance payments increase exposure when obligations remain incomplete.

## Risk Signals

- Large upfront payment before delivery or acceptance.
- Weak retention, bank guarantee, or recovery language.
- Final payment due before installation, commissioning, or training.
- Milestones based on vendor invoices rather than buyer acceptance.

## Follow-Up Questions

- What payment is due before delivery, installation, and acceptance?
- What security protects the buyer if the vendor fails to perform?
- Is there retention until successful commissioning and handover?
