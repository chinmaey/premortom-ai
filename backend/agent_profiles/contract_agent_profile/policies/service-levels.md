# Service-Level Review Guidance

## Rule

Service obligations should define uptime targets, response and resolution times, spare parts availability, escalation, exclusions, and remedies.

## Risk Signals

- Support terms promise availability without measurable response or resolution time.
- Spare parts, preventive maintenance, or consumables are unclear.
- Remedies for downtime are missing.
- Service coverage excludes local constraints likely to affect operations.

## Follow-Up Questions

- What response and resolution times apply?
- Are spare parts and preventive maintenance included?
- What remedy applies if uptime or service levels are missed?
