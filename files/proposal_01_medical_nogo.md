# Procurement Proposal — Vendor Submission

**Document type:** Vendor proposal / draft purchase order (PRE-APPROVAL)
**Buyer:** District Government Hospital, Radiology Department
**Reference:** DGH/RAD/MRI/2026-114

## 1. Item
Supply, installation and commissioning of one (1) 3.0 Tesla MRI Machine
(whole-body, 70 cm bore) including standard coils package.

## 2. Vendor
Meridian MedSystems Pvt Ltd (authorized channel partner). Registered office:
Mumbai. The vendor is the sole quoting party under the single-tender route
cited for "specialized equipment."

## 3. Commercials
- Total contract value: Rs 18.50 Crore (inclusive of installation).
- Payment terms: **70% advance on issue of Purchase Order**, 20% on delivery
  to site, 10% after installation sign-off.
- Price validity: 30 days. Prices firm; no benchmarking against alternate
  configurations provided.

## 4. Delivery & Installation
- Delivery: 16 weeks from advance receipt.
- Installation: by vendor team after site handover.
- Site status (per hospital engineering): MRI suite civil works approximately
  60% complete; RF shielding (Faraday cage) not yet started; dedicated power
  line and chiller/HVAC for magnet cooling pending procurement.

## 5. Warranty & Support
- Warranty: 24 months, **commencing from date of delivery to site.**
- No comprehensive annual maintenance contract (AMC) included beyond warranty;
  AMC to be negotiated separately later.
- No acceptance-testing / performance-qualification clause specified.

## 6. Staffing (buyer side)
- MRI technologists required: 2. Currently in post: 0 (recruitment not started).
- Radiologist: one shared across the district, not dedicated.

## 7. Vendor background notes (from buyer's file)
- Vendor was reportedly affected by a component supply-chain disruption in the
  last year affecting delivery timelines on two installations.
- One earlier supply contract with another buyer was terminated; reason not
  documented in file.

## 8. Justification offered
"Urgent clinical need; advance payment required by vendor to secure magnet
production slot. Site works expected to finish in time."
