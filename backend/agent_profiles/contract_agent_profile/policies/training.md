# Training Review Guidance

## Rule

Training obligations should identify audience, timing, materials, minimum competency, completion evidence, and retraining terms.

## Risk Signals

- Training is mentioned without schedule, audience, duration, or acceptance criteria.
- Training occurs before staff are hired or assigned.
- No obligation exists for repeat training after delayed commissioning.
- Handover is not linked to operator readiness.

## Follow-Up Questions

- Who must be trained before handover?
- What evidence confirms training completion?
- Is refresher or delayed-start training included?
