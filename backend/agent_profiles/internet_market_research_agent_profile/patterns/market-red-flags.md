# Market Research Red Flags

Watch for:

- Quote price materially outside visible market or public procurement range.
- Delivery timeline much shorter than normal without explanation.
- Advance payment much higher than common practice.
- Warranty start earlier than installation, commissioning, or acceptance norms.
- Training excluded when market norms indicate training is usually included.
- Service SLA missing resolution time, spare parts, uptime, preventive
  maintenance, remedies, or exclusions.
- Certification claims that are hard to verify.
- Public adverse news, debarment, litigation, or repeated delivery disputes.
- Vendor claims that conflict with public product/specification information.

These signals should be passed to the Bid Recommender Agent as benchmark context
and potential negotiation or human-review triggers.
