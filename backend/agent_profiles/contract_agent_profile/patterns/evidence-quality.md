# Evidence Quality Guidance

## Strong Evidence

- Direct clause text with clear responsibility, date, payment trigger, warranty term, or service obligation.
- Explicit acceptance, commissioning, training, or support requirement.

## Medium Evidence

- A clause implies responsibility but leaves timing or acceptance unclear.
- Procurement fields and proposal text agree, but clause text is incomplete.

## Weak Evidence

- Missing terms, indirect references, summaries, or inferred obligations.
- Conflicting fields without a source hierarchy.

## Output Guidance

Use strong evidence for findings. Use medium or weak evidence to frame follow-up questions and missing-term risks.
