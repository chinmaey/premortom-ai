# Procurement Proposal — Vendor Submission

**Document type:** Vendor quotation (PRE-APPROVAL)
**Buyer:** Corporate Facilities — Office Fit-out
**Reference:** FAC/FURN/2026-203

## 1. Item
Supply and installation of 40 ergonomic workstations (height-adjustable desks)
with task chairs, for an existing, operational office floor.

## 2. Vendor
Nationwide Office Solutions Ltd. Listed on the Government e-Marketplace (GeM)
with a strong fulfilment rating and no adverse records on file. Three
comparable vendor quotes were obtained; this was the lowest compliant bid.

## 3. Commercials
- Total value: Rs 0.22 Crore.
- Payment terms: 20% advance on PO, 80% on delivery and acceptance.
- Pricing benchmarked against two other GeM quotes; within market range.

## 4. Delivery
- Delivery and installation: 3 weeks from PO.

## 5. Site
- Office floor is operational and ready; floor plan and power/data points
  already in place. No civil or electrical work required.

## 6. Warranty & support
- Warranty: 36 months from installation, covering structure and mechanism.
- Standard manufacturer support; spares readily available.

## 7. Staffing
- Not applicable (commodity furniture; no specialized operation required).

## 8. Notes
Low-value, reversible commodity purchase to a ready site with standard terms
and a vetted vendor.
