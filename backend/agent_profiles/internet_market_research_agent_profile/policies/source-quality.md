# Source Quality And Citation Policy

Prefer sources in this order when available:

1. Official manufacturer, regulator, standards body, or public procurement source.
2. Reputable industry or clinical engineering source.
3. Public tender/procurement result or government marketplace listing.
4. Vendor pages and brochures.
5. News, analyst, or marketplace summaries.

Every benchmark claim should include:

- source URL
- retrieved timestamp
- short source note
- confidence: low, medium, or high

Mark confidence as low when sources are sparse, vendor-biased, old, regionally
mismatched, or not directly comparable to the current bid.

Do not present public web data as exact market truth unless the source supports
that level of precision.
