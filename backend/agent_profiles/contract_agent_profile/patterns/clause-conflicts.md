# Clause Conflict Patterns

## Conflict Types

- Different documents assign the same responsibility to different parties.
- A proposal promises a service that the contract excludes.
- Payment milestones conflict with acceptance or commissioning milestones.
- Warranty dates conflict across purchase order, vendor offer, and service agreement.

## Review Guidance

When conflicts appear, report them as contract ambiguity and explain why the ambiguity could cause delay, dispute, or financial exposure.
