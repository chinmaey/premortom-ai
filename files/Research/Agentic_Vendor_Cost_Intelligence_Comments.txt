My Assessment
The PPT research is about 80% correct, but it currently describes a Vendor Cost Intelligence platform, which many vendors partially address already.
For a hackathon-winning idea, shift the narrative to:
"PreMortem AI – The Agentic Decision Auditor that predicts procurement failures before approval."
That creates a much stronger differentiation than simply identifying vendor cost leakage.
